About me

Hi! My name is Rodrigo, but everyone calls me Roy. I was born in Madrid, Spain, and since I was a kid, I was in love with two things: gymnastics and technology, mainly because of how effortless and enjoyable they became while rising the level of its underlying complexity.

I got a degree in Sport Sciences in 2014, and for a few years I spent most of my time trying to become the best athlete I could be, which was fun. Nowadays you cand find me either in tech hackathons with one of my best friends, upside down every time I go practice, or making weird noises every time virtualy any animal crosses my way.

All in all, I started designing around 2015, and still learning something new every single day. I’m actively looking for an internship, or an entry-level UX job in order to keep growing.
