About me

Hi! I’m Rodrigo, but kids had a really hard time trying to spell the -dr- in my name, so now everyone calls me Roy. I was born in Madrid, Spain, and since I was a little hooman, I was in love with two things: gymnastics and technology.

I realized how much effort both worlds put into creating very complex “stuff”, and still make it look appealing and enjoyable. After quite a struggle deciding, I finally went for -and got- my degree in Sports Sciences, finishing in 2014.

But, a few years later, a friend of mine introduced me to the world of hackathons, and after participating in several of these events, I realized how much I loved designing products and user experiences, with ideas aimed at making people feel safer, or healthier.
