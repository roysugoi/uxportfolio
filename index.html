About me

Hi! My name is Rodrigo, but everyone calls me Roy. I was born in Madrid, Spain, and since I was a kid, I was in love with two things: gymnastics and technology.

Although they might seem very different, I was constantly mesmerized whenever I thought how both worlds would continuously bring complexity to a whole new level, and yet making it feel much more simple, effortless and enjoyable every time.

I started designing around 2015, and I’m still learning something new every single day. I’m actively looking for an internship, or an entry-level UX job to keep growing.
