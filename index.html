About me

Hi! I’m Rodrigo, but kids had a really hard time trying to spell the -dr- in my name, so now everyone calls me Roy.

I was born in Madrid, Spain, and since I was a little hooman, I was in love with two things: gymnastics and technology. Although they might seem like very different worlds, they just have this thing in common: they pour so much effort into taking very complex “stuff” and make it look easy.

And I find that waaaay too interesting.
