About me

Hi! I’m Rodrigo, but kids had a really hard time trying to spell the “dr” in my name, so now everyone calls me Roy. I was born in Madrid, Spain, and since I was a kid, I was in love with two things: gymnastics and technology.

Although they might seem very different, I was constantly mesmerized whenever I thought how both worlds would continuously bring complexity to a whole new level, and yet making it feel much more simple, effortless and enjoyable every time.

So, I had to decide! I ended up getting a degree in Sports Sciences in 2014, and shortly after I graduated, I discovered tech hackathons thanks to a friend of mine. I absolutely love them, and they made me realize how badly I wanted to become a UX designer. And here I am.

I’m still learning something new every single day, and I’m actively looking for an internship, or an entry-level UX job to keep growing.

Thank you for visiting, and if you want to contact me you can do so by clicking here.
